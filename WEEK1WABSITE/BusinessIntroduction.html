Game Production and Distribution:

Millennium Game Company is a company dedicated to game development and publishing. We have a passionate and creative game design team committed to developing captivating game content. Through marketing and promotional strategies, we strive to ensure that each game attracts its target audience and stands out in the competitive gaming industry.

Production of Gaming Peripheral Products:

Millennium Game Company is not just a gaming company; we also specialize in the production of gaming-related peripherals. From personalized game-themed apparel to practical gaming accessories, our peripheral products are designed to add more fun and personality to your gaming world. Through continuous innovation and the provision of high-quality games and peripherals, we are committed to becoming your preferred gaming partner.

Education and Training:

In the field of education and training, our gaming company is committed to sharing professional knowledge and experience in the gaming industry, empowering individuals and teams to succeed in this exhilarating field. We provide multi-level education and training services designed to meet the diverse needs of students at different levels and with various interests. We offer design philosophy workshops that delve into memorable elements in games and explore how to create captivating gaming experiences. Regularly organizing events, we foster communication and interaction among players.
